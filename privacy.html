Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Solict ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

  • Server Information: Server IDs, server names, and server configuration settings
  • Channel Information: Channel IDs and names for features like logging and announcements
  • User Information: User IDs and usernames for command execution and moderation features
  • Command Usage: Commands used and their parameters for functionality and improvement purposes
  • Message Content: Limited message content for moderation features (auto-moderation, message logging)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the Bot's core functionalities
  • Process commands and deliver requested features
  • Improve and optimize the Bot's performance
  • Provide moderation and server management tools
  • Troubleshoot issues and provide technical support

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only. We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection features
  • Remove the Bot from your server at any time

7. Third-Party Services

Solict may integrate with third-party services (e.g., Discord API). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

8. Children's Privacy

Solict complies with Discord's minimum age requirements (13+ or 16+ in certain regions). We do not knowingly collect data from users under these age limits.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through our support server at https://discord.gg/5ry3HysZ7E.
